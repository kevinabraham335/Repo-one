IEEE CS

IEEE Computer Society has long been stationed among the garrisons of computing power and knowledge that humankind has come to accumulate till date. Our student chapter is one among 200 such organizations worldwide, and we strive to look at everything from a perspective that is always deemed different.

At VIT, this society aims at creating awareness about the new advances in the field of Computer Science and imparting knowledge related to this field by conducting different workshops and events throughout the semester. It gives substantial benefit to all the computer enthusiasts as well as provides a support to other students to know more about the technology that is ruling the world today.
